Booking

It is possible to stay at our farm throughout the year, except between 16th January and 31th January, which is the period within which we reward ourselves with our vacation. On the day you arrive your room will be available after 4 pm and your time of vacation is before 10 am on the morning of your departure. Breakfast is served between 8 and 10 am, although if you desire an early departure, we can usually accommodate your needs for an earlier breakfast on request. The cost of your stay can be easily calculated using the formula according to your choice of accommodation, adding any additional services, and allowing for the seasons of the year.Your booking can only be confirmed once we have received your payment of 30% of the total cost of your stay, which must be received by us within 15 days of your booking. If we don't receive your required payment within that time, please understand that your booking and quote automatically expires and we cannot guarantee your accommodation because we may have other bookings, which will take precedence.
